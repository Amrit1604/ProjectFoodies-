FAQ - Food Ordering Site

How do I place an order?
To place an order, simply browse our menu, select the items you want, and proceed to checkout. Follow the instructions to complete your order.
Can I modify my order after placing it?
Once an order is placed, modifications are not allowed. Please double-check your order before confirming.
How can I track my order?
Once your order is confirmed, you will receive a tracking link via email or SMS. You can use that link to track the status of your order in real-time.
Is there a minimum order amount?
Yes, we have a minimum order amount requirement. Please check the details on our website or app.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept various payment methods, including credit cards, debit cards, and online payment services. Check our payment options during checkout.
Can I cancel my order?
Cancellation policies vary. You can review our cancellation policy on the checkout page before confirming your order.
Are there delivery charges?
Delivery charges may apply and are calculated based on your delivery location. Check the delivery charges during the checkout process.
Do you offer catering services?
Yes, we offer catering services for events and gatherings. Contact our customer support for more details and customization options.
How do I provide feedback on my order?
We value your feedback. You can provide feedback through our website, app, or by contacting our customer support team.
Do you have vegetarian and vegan options?
Yes, we offer a variety of vegetarian and vegan options. You can find them labeled on our menu.
How do I create an account?
To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" or "Register" link on our website or app. Follow the instructions to provide the required information and create your account.
What should I do if I have allergies?
If you have allergies, please check the allergen information provided with each menu item. Additionally, you can contact our customer support for assistance.
